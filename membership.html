Common Questions

Everything you need to know before joining

Is there a joining fee or contract? ▼
No joining fee and no long-term contracts. All our memberships are month-to-month. You can cancel with 30 days' notice at any time, no questions asked.
How does the 7-day free trial work? ▼
Sign up via our contact form or front desk. You'll get full Elite-level access for 7 days absolutely free. No credit card required to start. At the end of your trial, choose the plan that suits you.
Can I freeze my membership? ▼
Yes. Elite and Champion members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year at no cost — perfect for travel or injury recovery. Basic members can freeze for 1 month per year.
Are there discounts for students or couples? ▼
Yes! We offer 15% off for verified students (ID required), couples who join together get 10% off both memberships, and corporate rates are available for teams of 5+. Contact us for details.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept eSewa, Khalti, FonePay, bank transfer, cash, and all major credit/debit cards. Memberships are billed on the same date each month.
