About IronForge

Our Story · Our Mission · Our People

12

Built From Sweat & Sacrifice

IronForge Gym was founded in 2012 by former national powerlifting champion Utsav Shrestha in a 600 sq/ft basement with 8 barbells and a dream. Today, we're Nepal's most decorated fitness facility — spanning 18,000 sq/ft across three floors in the heart of Kathmandu.

What hasn't changed in 12 years is our philosophy: every single person who walks through our doors is capable of extraordinary things. We're here to prove it.

We've trained Olympians, CEOs, first-timers, and everyone in between. The bar doesn't care about your background — and neither do we.
